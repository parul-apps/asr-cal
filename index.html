Thank you for choosing ASR Calculator. Your privacy is our top priority. This policy outlines how your data is handled and protected when using the ASR Calculator app. By continuing to use our app, you consent to the practices described here.

Introduction

ASR Calculator is a comprehensive app designed to help users perform calculations ranging from basic arithmetic to advanced mathematical functions like trigonometry. This document will explain what data is handled, if any, and how your privacy remains protected while using the app.

Data Collection and Privacy

Your privacy is guaranteed. ASR Calculator does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All your calculations occur directly on your device, without any external data transfer. None of your input data is shared or stored elsewhere.

Core Features

The key functions of ASR Calculator include:

  • Arithmetic: Basic operations like addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.
  • Advanced Functions: Including trigonometric functions (sin, cos, tan), logarithms, roots, powers, and factorials.
  • Dynamic UI: Auto-adjusts font size based on the length of your input for easy readability.
  • Error Handling: Provides clear messages for incorrect or unsupported inputs like large factorials.
  • No Permissions: The app does not require any device permissions, ensuring complete user privacy.

Technology Stack

ASR Calculator is built using the following technologies:

  • Kotlin: A modern, efficient programming language.
  • Android Studio: Development environment for building the app.
  • AndroidX Libraries: Used for modern UI features and app compatibility.
  • Material Components: For a consistent and appealing user interface.
  • BigInteger (Java Math Library): For handling large numerical calculations such as factorials.

Permissions

ASR Calculator requires no permissions on your device. Specifically, we do not access the following:

  • No Personal Data: The app does not store or transmit any personal information.
  • No Location: We do not track or request access to your location.
  • No Internet Access: The app operates entirely offline, ensuring your data stays local.
  • No Access to Contacts or Files: ASR Calculator does not interact with your contacts, camera, or files.

Error Handling

The app is designed to notify users when errors occur. Some examples include:

  • Division by Zero: Users are alerted that division by zero is not allowed.
  • Invalid Inputs: The app displays error messages for unsupported formats or characters.
  • Large Factorials: For large numbers, factorial calculations are limited to prevent performance issues.
